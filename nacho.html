No. The course is self-paced and will be broken down into short pre-recorded video segments with periodic assessments to check on your progress.

What do I receive for taking this course?

You will receive a final grade but the grade won't be on your course completion confirmation. To receive a certificate of completion, you will need to achieve 65% on graded material. Services Provided & Rates

First Time Treatment

  Includes a full evaluation of your holistic needs Fill out an intake form; Discuss your treatment plan; Assess treatment goals. First treatment adds an additional 30 minutes to ensure enough time to discuss other services and options that are available including exercises, aromatherapy and craniosacral.

What to Expect during Your First Visit

Your first massage treatment will take about 90-120 minutes depending on massage duration chosen. It is advised to come showered and dressed in loose fit clothing. Please arrive a couple minutes before session start time to allow yourself to settle and use the restroom if necessary. Before your treatment begins, we will discuss your intake form, perform a few diagnostic tests and discuss your treatment plan. At the end of the session, we will discuss any recommendations suitable for your personal wellness.

(30-minute consultation + 60-minute treatment): $90
(30-minute consultation + 90-minute treatment): $140

Therapeutic Treatment

Combination of deep tissue, acupressure, stretching and ART (active release technique) to focus on relieving tight musculature, chronic pain, and injury. Techniques may range from subtle to intense sensations depending on ailment. Each treatment will aim to release and relax tissue addressing overused musculature, prolonged tension and/or acute and continuous pain.

Relaxation Treatment

Restorative treatment that combines acupressure, nourishing holds, and soothing strokes to relax the body, ease the mind and rejuvenate the soul. Treatment includes a choice of aromatherapy and choice of music for a personalized experience.

Massage Cupping (add on)  $5

Suction cupping additional to treatment that helps remove stagnation, loosen adhesions and knotted connective tissue by stimulating circulation and blood flow. Cups can be placed on back, glutes, legs, chest and/or shoulders while massaging or stretching other areas. Cups may be used throughout duration of session being placed in different areas or up to 10 minutes on a concentrated area.
